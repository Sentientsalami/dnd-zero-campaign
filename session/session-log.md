### Session 1 — 14th of Kythorn, 1492 DR
**Title:** Lady Luck's Cut
**Location(s):** Trades Ward, Dock Ward (The Blushing Mermaid), Southern Ward (Wheel Street), Sea Ward (New Olamn), Castle Ward (The Yawning Portal)

**Summary:**
- Zero, mid-con in the Trades Ward as Sera's baker's cousin, spotted New Olamn alumnus Aldric Fenn being escorted under obvious duress toward the Dock Ward by two plainly-dressed heavies. Zero finished the con against cloth merchant Dorvil, then tailed the group.
- Tracked Aldric and his escorts to the Blushing Mermaid, a notorious Dock Ward tavern with Xanathar Guild connections. Shifted into Simon and changed into rougher clothes before entering.
- At the bar, established a cover identity as a Luskan drifter with the bartender Hess. Eavesdropped on a meeting between Aldric and a woman named Lira — a collector/broker with deep Dock Ward connections — who gave Aldric three days to retrieve a music box he had moved without her knowledge, or face unspecified consequences.
- After Lira and her men departed, Zero approached Aldric in cover and covertly identified themselves. Aldric explained the situation: he'd purchased a mysterious antique music box at the Melvaunt estate auction, Lira's people had come for it almost immediately, and he'd panicked and given it to a former intimate, Rynn Ashvale, for safekeeping. He offered fourteen gold for its retrieval.
- Zero scouted Rynn's location — a room above a cooperage on Wheel Street in the Southern Ward — then approached her front door as Simon with a cover story: Simon was the cousin of a recently deceased woman named Felicia Grey, and the music box had sentimental value. Rynn, visibly carrying her own complicated feelings about whoever had left the box with her, sold it for one gold without significant resistance.
- Zero spent thirty minutes on a counter-surveillance walk through the Southern Ward before proceeding to New Olamn, where they returned the box to Aldric and collected fourteen gold. Net gain: thirteen gold.
- Zero shifted into Sebastian and spent the afternoon at the Yawning Portal, where they inserted themselves into an argument between three expedition members — scholar Mira, field veteran Daven, and tiefling patron Corvin Ashgate — over a map of the Dessarin Valley. Zero navigated the dispute, impressed the group with Sebastian's presence, and was offered a position on a three-week expedition to Vaester's Tower: forty gold on departure, forty on return. Planning meeting set for one week's time at the same table.

**Key Decisions Made:**
- Zero chose to break off the Dorvil con and follow Aldric immediately, then doubled back to finish the con before tailing — a calculated risk that paid off but cost some visual on Aldric.
- Zero took Aldric's job without asking too many questions and delivered cleanly, establishing a working relationship with a New Olamn alumnus who now owes them significantly.
- Zero deliberately did not examine the music box at length or attempt to open it before delivering it. They prioritized payment over curiosity. The box's contents and the nature of what Lira actually wants remain unknown.
- Zero signed onto the Dessarin expedition as Sebastian — a persona with no established prior history. This may require maintenance.

**NPCs Met:**
- Aldric Fenn — Human male, New Olamn alumnus, lute player, mid-twenties. Nervous, genuine, gifted. Got in over his head purchasing a mysterious music box at auction. Owes Zero a significant debt.
- Lira — Human female, lean, dark-haired, sharp-featured. Dock Ward broker and collector operating on behalf of unknown parties. Calm, patient, and dangerous. Possible Xanathar Guild connections.
- Lira's Two Men — unnamed, plainly dressed, physically imposing. Muscle only. No names established.
- Hess — Half-orc female, bartender at the Blushing Mermaid. Efficient, observant, professionally incurious. Not hostile.
- Rynn Ashvale — Human female, mid-twenties. Southern Ward busker, lap harp player, genuinely skilled. Sharp features, ink-stained fingers, small scar through right eyebrow. Unknowingly held the music box for Aldric. Has complicated unresolved feelings about him. Sold the box without significant resistance.
- Corvin Ashgate — Tiefling male, approximately twenty, lavender skin, small swept-back horns. Well-dressed. Finances the Dessarin expedition. Open, warm, somewhat self-deprecating about his role. Carries a calling card with the Ashgate family seal.
- Mira — Human female, forties, iron-grey hair, ink-stained fingers. Scholar and academic lead of the Dessarin expedition. Precise, exacting, not unfriendly once she's decided you're useful.
- Daven — Human male, older, lean and weathered, mostly-grey beard. Field lead of the expedition. Has personally visited the Dessarin Valley and the area around Vaester's Tower. Terse, perceptive, respects directness.

**Loot / Rewards:**
- 14 GP from Aldric Fenn (retrieval payment)
- Corvin Ashgate's calling card (Ashgate family seal — potential social capital)
- 80 GP promised (40 on departure, 40 on return) for the Dessarin expedition
- Information: The music box was purchased at the Melvaunt estate auction (minor noble family, last of the line died Nightal 1491 DR). It contains a hidden compartment, a thirteen-point compass rose inlay, and script in an unidentified language. Lira and her principal want it badly enough to threaten a music student.
- Information: Vaester's Tower, a pre-Netherese ruin, is the expedition's destination in the Dessarin Valley. The north face approach is blocked by rockfall of unknown date.

**Notes for Next Session:**
- The Dorvil con's financial outcome was never resolved. The deposit and receipt situation should be addressed — either Zero reclaimed the deposit successfully (off-screen) or the con is a loose end.
- Aldric has three days from the 14th of Kythorn (so until the 17th) to deliver the music box to Lira. Zero is not involved in that delivery, but may hear about the outcome. If the delivery goes wrong, it could resurface.

### Session 2 — 15th–18th of Kythorn, 1492 DR
**Title:** The First Song
**Location(s):** Trades Ward, North Ward (Torven Ashwick's townhouse, The Velvet Alcove), Castle Ward (Renaer Neverember's townhouse, The Yawning Portal), Sea Ward (New Olamn), Southern Ward, various streets

**Summary:**
- Zero resolved the Dorvil con cleanly, deploying the persona of Gregory Hamith (Guild Factor) to extract twenty gold from the cloth merchant via forged paperwork. Net on the con: 20 GP, 5 SP.
- Returned to New Olamn to visit Aldric Fenn, discovered him transcribing a melody from the music box lid — identified by Zero as Netherese resonance mapping tradition. Together, Zero and Aldric opened the Instrument using the Lydian augmented interval and the Weave, revealing the First Key (a dark metal disc) and a second vellum in Common directing them to three Keys needed to restore "what was unmade" — the first song, silenced before the first civilization arose.
- Zero brought Aldric and the Instrument to retired scholar Torven Ashwick, whose partial Loross translation confirmed the instrument's nature and pointed toward the Dessarin Valley — specifically the ruins of Netherese enclave Gharraghaur, beneath Vaester's Tower.
- Zero secured Force Grey backing from Renaer Neverember: plainclothes protection for Torven's house, a surveillance asset (Petra) on Zero, and organizational support against Lira's principal. In exchange: a full briefing before any significant action.
- Aldric placed in protective custody at The Velvet Alcove (35 GP personal funds) for three days, playing parlour lute and remaining unfindable.
- Full expedition coalition assembled: Zero, Aldric, Corvin, Mira, Daven, Torven, and field assets Sable (ranger) and Brynn Coldmantle (dwarf cleric, Moradin).
- Expedition permits filed as Academic Survey with the Surveyors' Guild (12 GP expedition fund). Provisions procured from Halvern's Dry Goods (35 GP), Brennan's Apothecary (18 GP), and Harwick's Equipment (20 GP — discounted from 24 GP after Zero encouraged Harwick's son Edder toward bardic study).
- On the 17th (Lira's deadline), Force Grey asset Petra confirmed Lira is moving to a new plan — not angry, already adapting. A hooded figure bearing a thirteen-point compass rose met with Lira that morning and departed north.
- Zero followed compass rose markers through the North Ward to a courtyard meeting with Syrenne — a Mulhorandi musicologist and former student of Torven's who has spent eleven years independently tracking the Instrument. Syrenne revealed that Torven Ashwick is Lira's principal, confirmed by an authentic twelve-year-old letter in Torven's hand referencing "leverage" over the resonance network.
- Zero briefed Daven privately (as Simon). Daven briefed Corvin. Mira being briefed separately. Consensus: watch Torven, say nothing, act if necessary.
- Syrenne introduced to the full team on departure morning. Torven and Syrenne had a charged recognition moment; Zero redirected immediately. Syrenne joins the expedition.
- Lira's tail intercepted one block from Corvin's townhouse on departure morning — a young, inexperienced asset. Zero intimidated him off with a quiet threat. Party departed Waterdeep 18th of Kythorn, full complement, road north.

**Key Decisions Made:**
- Zero chose to open the Instrument rather than leave it sealed — a decision that set the entire session's events in motion.
- Zero brought Aldric into the expedition coalition rather than sidelining him, on the basis that the Instrument chose its openers.
- Zero chose Force Grey (Renaer Neverember) over the Harpers or Zhentarim for organizational backing — pragmatism over ideology.
- Zero chose to leverage Torven rather than expose or remove him — keeping a potentially compromised asset in play under observation.
- Zero recruited Syrenne on the basis of demonstrated knowledge, personal integrity under Insight scrutiny, and the authenticated letter — accepting that trust must be earned on the road.
- Zero told Corvin's Sebastian persona the full truth, then revealed their true identity to Corvin directly. Trust established on both sides.

**NPCs Met:**
- Syrenne — Mulhorandi musicologist, fifties, eleven years tracking the Instrument independently. Former student of Torven's. No organizational affiliation. Carries a thirteen-point compass rose. Believes Torven is Lira's principal; has authenticated letter as evidence. Joins the expedition.
- Sable — Human ranger, forties, dark-skinned, quiet and watchful. Daven's field contact. Knows the Dessarin Valley intimately. Joins the expedition.
- Brynn Coldmantle — Dwarf cleric of Moradin, broad, warm, direct. Daven's field contact. Good in a fight, better in a ruin. Joins the expedition.
- Petra — Force Grey surveillance asset, Renaer's operative. Thirties, unremarkable appearance, professional. Made contact with Zero in the Castle Ward Market on the 17th.
- Pell — Stout, shaved-head barkeep at Felzoun's Folly. Reliable.
- Bram — Tethyrian sailor, broad-shouldered, laugh-lined. In port four days. Good company, no complications.
- Halvern — Barrel-shaped proprietress of Halvern's Dry Goods. Drives a hard bargain, respects a good pitch.
- Brennan — Gnome apothecary. Precise, professional. Wants Dessarin Valley botanical specimens to Mira's specification.
- Harwick — Former soldier turned equipment merchant. Doesn't negotiate on price. Has a son named Edder (16) who wants to be a bard.
- Edder Harwick — 16, gangly, dark-haired. Lute player of genuine potential, held back by self-doubt. Zero broke through it. Told to find Zero at New Olamn.
- Cassia — Half-elf jeweler. Sharp-tongued, excellent company, strong opinions. Two-night acquaintance.
- Vanna — Proprietress of The Velvet Alcove. Runs a tight, discreet house. Owes Zero a social debt; repaid with Aldric's three-day sanctuary.
- Ossian — Corvin's Calishite manservant. Unflappable, indispensable, anticipates everything.
