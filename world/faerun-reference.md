# Faerûn World Reference

*Broad lore, geography, and context for adventuring beyond Waterdeep.*

---

## The Sword Coast (Overview)
A stretch of coastline along the western edge of Faerûn, running roughly from Luskan in the north to Baldur's Gate and Amn in the south. The most heavily trafficked and adventured region in the setting.

### Major Cities (North to South)
| City | Notes |
|---|---|
| Luskan | "City of Sails." Pirate city, ruled by the Arcane Brotherhood. Dangerous. |
| Neverwinter | "The City That Was." Recovering from the Spellplague and volcanic eruption. Lord Neverember has rebuilt much of it. |
| Waterdeep | City of Splendors. Most powerful city-state on the coast. Zero's home base. |
| Baldur's Gate | Major port and trade city. Grand Dukes rule. Tension between the wealthy and the poor (the Outer City). |
| Candlekeep | Great library-fortress. Entrance requires donating a book not in their collection. |

### Inland Regions
| Region | Notes |
|---|---|
| The Dessarin Valley | Rural, west of the Sword Mountains. Site of many adventures. |
| Silverymoon | Northern cultural capital. Scholarly, magical, prosperous. Home to the Lady's College. |
| Mithral Hall | Dwarven stronghold beneath the Spine of the World. |
| Anauroch | The great desert to the east. Once the Netherese empire. |

## Major Factions (Broader Faerûn)

| Faction | Base | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Harpers | Distributed | Agents of good, oppose tyranny and the accumulation of power |
| Zhentarim | Zhentil Keep / distributed | Mercenary network, seek to control trade routes |
| Lords' Alliance | Waterdeep-led | Coalition of northern cities |
| Red Wizards of Thay | Thay (east) | Powerful, dangerous, seek to dominate magic |
| Arcane Brotherhood | Luskan | Powerful wizard organization, controlling Luskan |
| The Cult of the Dragon | Distributed | Seek to create dracoliches and draconic undead |
