# Waterdeep District Guide

*Canon reference for the City of Splendors. Zero's home base.*

---

## Overview
Waterdeep (pop. ~130,000) is the most powerful city-state on the Sword Coast and one of the greatest cities in all Faerûn. Governed by the enigmatic Masked Lords and their public face, the Open Lord. The city is built on and around Mount Waterdeep, overlooking the Sea of Swords.

**Current Open Lord:** Laeral Silverhand
**City Watch:** The Watch (city law enforcement)
**City Guard:** The Guard (military, gates, walls)

---

## The Wards

### Castle Ward
The civic and political heart of the city. Home to Castle Waterdeep (seat of government), Piergeiron's Palace, and the Market. Central and relatively safe.

### Sea Ward
The wealthiest residential district. Home to the nobility, grand temples, and the **Field of Triumph** (arena). Also home to **New Olamn**, the bardic college Zero attended.
- *Key locations:* New Olamn, Temple of Sune, Temple of Lathander, Tower of the Order

### North Ward
Upper-middle-class residential. Quieter than most wards. Home to many merchants and guildmasters.

### Trades Ward
The commercial hub. Packed with shops, markets, and guild halls. Busy around the clock.
- *Key locations:* The Court of the White Bull, numerous guild halls

### Dock Ward
Rough, working-class, and dangerous after dark. Sailors, dockworkers, smugglers, and the city's criminal underbelly. The Xanathar Guild has significant influence here.
- *Key locations:* The harbor, Fish Street, numerous taverns and flophouses

### Southern Ward (South Ward)
Working-class residential. Laborers and tradespeople. Less dangerous than Dock Ward but not glamorous.

### City of the Dead
A large, walled cemetery district. Surprisingly pleasant by day — locals picnic here. After dark it's locked and patrolled. Undead occasionally cause problems despite wards.

### Undermountain / Skullport
Below the city. Undermountain is the infamous megadungeon beneath Mount Waterdeep. Skullport is a hidden port city deep below, a haven for slavers and criminals.

## New Olamn (Zero's College)
Located in the Sea Ward. One of the most prestigious bardic colleges on the Sword Coast, rebuilt in the late 1300s DR after the original was destroyed. Teaches music, history, lore, and performance. Alumni form a loose but loyal network throughout the city and beyond.
