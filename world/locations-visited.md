# Locations Visited

*Running log of every significant place Zero has been. Brief notes for continuity.*

---

## Waterdeep

### The Blushing Mermaid
**Ward:** Dock Ward
**Type:** Tavern (and more)
**First Visited:** Session 1
**Description:** A notorious Dock Ward fixture accessible via a narrow passage off Fish Street, descending stone steps to the waterline. Low ceilings, deliberate darkness, and the kind of clientele that values privacy over company. Not a place you go for the ale.
**Key NPCs here:** Hess (bartender), Lira (met here Session 1), Lira's two unnamed men, Aldric Fenn
**Notes:** Widely understood to have Xanathar Guild connections. Zero entered as Simon and was not made. Lira uses it for meetings — she had a reserved table in the back corner and arrived ahead of Aldric.

---

### Rynn Ashvale's Room
**Ward:** Southern Ward
**Type:** Residential (rented room above cooperage)
**First Visited:** Session 1
**Description:** A small, tidy single room above a cooperage on Wheel Street. Cot, writing desk, a crate for a table. Rynn busks for income and practices here.
**Key NPCs here:** Rynn Ashvale
**Notes:** The music box was stored here on Aldric's request without Rynn's full knowledge of what it was or why. Accessed via an exterior wooden staircase on the building's south side.

---

### New Olamn
**Ward:** Sea Ward
**Type:** Bardic College
**First Visited:** Session 1 (Zero is an alumnus — long familiarity)
**Description:** A handsome stone building with tall windows and carved doors depicting bardic history. One of the most prestigious bardic colleges on the Sword Coast.
**Key NPCs here:** Aldric Fenn (alumnus, currently on expedition); Edder Harwick (prospective student, directed here by Zero)
**Notes:** Zero's alma mater. Zero has alumni standing here — doors open, faces recognized. Lira's men entered the college openly to collect Aldric in Session 1, suggesting either confidence or Dock Ward reach extending into the Sea Ward.

---

### The Yawning Portal
**Ward:** Castle Ward
**Type:** Inn and Tavern
**First Visited:** Session 1
**Description:** A famous round-tower inn built over the entrance to Undermountain. The well drops into the megadungeon below and occasionally exhales cool air from the dark. Good afternoon crowd of adventurers, merchants, and locals.
**Key NPCs here:** Corvin Ashgate, Mira, Daven
**Notes:** Zero (as Sebastian) met the Dessarin expedition team here. Planning meeting scheduled for 21st of Kythorn at the same table. Sebastian is now a recognized face to all three expedition members.

---

## Outside Waterdeep

### The Sleeping Griffon
**Region:** Rassalantar, North Road
**Type:** Roadside inn
**First Visited:** Session 3
**Description:** A roadside waystation inn in the small settlement of Rassalantar, approximately one day's travel north of Waterdeep. Unremarkable but clean. The griffon on the sign looks sedated.
**Key NPCs here:** Unnamed barkeep (Neverwinter accent, practical, well-informed)
**Notes:** Zero gathered useful road intelligence here — Sergeant Brandart's profile, the coffle attack past the Stone Bridge, and the recommendation to visit the Mielikki shrine.

---

### The Horses and the Hills
**Region:** Amphail, North Road
**Type:** Inn and tavern
**First Visited:** Session 3
**Description:** A clean, reliable roadside inn in Amphail. Exactly what Daven promised — unremarkable and good. Long common room, decent hearth.
**Key NPCs here:** Cort (proprietor, magnificent grey moustache)
**Notes:** Zero negotiated entertainment for reduced lodging. Zero and Aldric performed together — a significant musical session including Aldric's original slow piece debuted publicly for the first time. Cost: 14 GP for nine people (discounted from 18 GP). Zero is welcome back.

---

### Amphail Checkpoint
**Region:** Amphail crossroads, North Road
**Type:** Road checkpoint
**First Visited:** Session 3
**Description:** A folding table, a ledger, and a young sergeant who takes his work seriously. Helm medallion at his throat.
**Key NPCs here:** Sergeant Brandart
**Notes:** Party cleared the checkpoint with eight members listed on the Academic Survey permit; Syrenne turned invisible. Brandart noted the empty space beside Zero and let it go. He is thorough but satisfiable with correct paperwork.

---

### The Stone Bridge
**Region:** Dessarin Valley, Dessarin River crossing
**Type:** Bridge (dwarven construction)
**First Visited:** Session 3
**Description:** An old, low-arched bridge of good dwarven stonework spanning the Dessarin River. Older than the road it carries. The river runs cold and quick beneath it with snowmelt.
**Key NPCs here:** None permanent
**Notes:** Sable was dispatched here to double back and investigate the thessalar tracks while the party continued north to the Mielikki shrine.

---

### Shrine of Mielikki
**Region:** Dessarin Valley, approximately one mile north of the Stone Bridge
**Type:** Wilderness shrine, forester waypoint
**First Visited:** Session 3
**Description:** A natural clearing in old growth that feels chosen rather than cleared. A standing stone carved with Mielikki's symbol wound round with living ivy. Offerings at its base — wildflowers, feathers, small carved animals. Maintained by a small community of foresters who know the valley intimately.
**Key NPCs here:** Unnamed forester (forties, weathered, longbow)
**Notes:** The foresters have watched the thessalar for years and confirmed it hasn't ranged past the Stone Bridge in eleven years. Provided the secondary entrance location to Gharraghaur: cave mouth, western face, below Vaester's Tower foundations. Party camped here overnight on the 19th of Kythorn. Brennan the apothecary apparently sends word ahead when interesting parties leave Waterdeep.
