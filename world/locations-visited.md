# Locations Visited

*Running log of every significant place Zero has been. Brief notes for continuity.*

## Waterdeep

### The Blushing Mermaid
**Ward:** Dock Ward
**Type:** Tavern (and more)
**First Visited:** Session 1
**Description:** A notorious Dock Ward fixture accessible via a narrow passage off Fish Street, descending stone steps to the waterline. Low ceilings, deliberate darkness, and the kind of clientele that values privacy over company. Not a place you go for the ale.
**Key NPCs here:** Hess (bartender), Lira (met here Session 1), Lira's two unnamed men, Aldric Fenn
**Notes:** Widely understood to have Xanathar Guild connections. Zero entered as Simon and was not made. Lira uses it for meetings — she had a reserved table in the back corner and arrived ahead of Aldric.

---

### Rynn Ashvale's Room
**Ward:** Southern Ward
**Type:** Residential (rented room above cooperage)
**First Visited:** Session 1
**Description:** A small, tidy single room above a cooperage on Wheel Street. Cot, writing desk, a crate for a table. Rynn busks for income and practices here.
**Key NPCs here:** Rynn Ashvale
**Notes:** The music box was stored here on Aldric's request without Rynn's full knowledge of what it was or why. Accessed via an exterior wooden staircase on the building's south side.

---

### New Olamn
**Ward:** Sea Ward
**Type:** Bardic College
**First Visited:** Session 1 (Zero is an alumna — long familiarity)
**Description:** A handsome stone building with tall windows and carved doors depicting bardic history. One of the most prestigious bardic colleges on the Sword Coast.
**Key NPCs here:** Aldric Fenn (resident/student)
**Notes:** Zero's alma mater. Zero has alumni standing here — doors open, faces recognized. Lira's men entered the college openly to collect Aldric, suggesting either confidence or Dock Ward reach extending into the Sea Ward.

---

### The Yawning Portal
**Ward:** Castle Ward
**Type:** Inn and Tavern
**First Visited:** Session 1
**Description:** A famous round-tower inn built over the entrance to Undermountain. The well drops into the megadungeon below and occasionally exhales cool air from the dark. Good afternoon crowd of adventurers, merchants, and locals.
**Key NPCs here:** Corvin Ashgate, Mira, Daven
**Notes:** Zero (as Sebastian) met the Dessarin expedition team here. Planning meeting scheduled for 21st of Kythorn at the same table. Sebastian is now a recognized face to all three expedition members.
