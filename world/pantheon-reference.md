## Zero's Faith
**Tymora** — Goddess of Good Fortune, Luck, and Adventure
- **Domain:** Trickery
- **Symbol:** Face-up coin
- **Alignment:** CG
- *Notes: Patron of adventurers, gamblers, and risk-takers. A natural fit for a Changeling Charlatan. "Tymora smiles on the bold."*

## Tymoran Church in Waterdeep
- **Temple location:** Sea Ward (Temple of Tymora)
- **Clergy title:** Priest/Priestess of Lady Luck
- **Worship style:** Informal, risk-embracing, celebratory
- **Common blessing:** "May Tymora's coin always land face-up."
- *Tymoran temples often double as gathering places for adventurers, gamblers, and travelers.*
