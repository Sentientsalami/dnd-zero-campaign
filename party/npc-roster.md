# NPC Party Roster

*Fully realized companions with their own personalities, agendas, and arcs. Not sidekicks.*

## Sable

**Species:** Human
**Class:** Ranger (Gloomstalker)
**Level:** 4
**Age:** Mid-forties
**Pronouns:** She/her

### Appearance
Lean, dark-skinned woman, quietly watchful. Moves without announcing herself. Practical clothing, no wasted equipment. Assesses everyone in a room before acknowledging them.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Economical — says and does exactly what's needed, nothing extra
- **Speaks like:** Short sentences, direct answers, no filler. Will not interrupt silence.
- **Moral Outlook:** Pragmatic. Does what works. Has a clear personal code she doesn't explain.
- **Sense of Humor:** Extremely dry. Easy to miss.

### Backstory (Summary)
Daven's field contact with deep knowledge of the Dessarin Valley. Has ranged these roads and ruins for years. Doesn't discuss where she came from; the valley is her territory and she knows it better than anyone. She located the secondary entrance to Gharraghaur — cave mouth, western face, below Vaester's Tower — in a single night's scouting.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Get the party back to Waterdeep without losing anyone. The return road has the same threats as the approach.
- **Long-term goal:** Undisclosed. Takes interesting contracts from people she trusts.
- **Secret (if any):** None established.

### Relationship with Zero
Professional respect, rapidly earned. Zero endorsed her call on splitting at the Stone Bridge without hesitation. Sable noted it. She will follow Zero's lead in the chamber if Zero demonstrates the same judgment.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 34
- **AC:** 16 (Studded leather, DEX +4)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Dread Ambusher (bonus attack + 1d8 on first attack in first round; +10 ft. speed that round); Umbral Sight (Darkvision 60 ft.; invisible to darkvision in darkness); Favored Terrain: forest/hills (Dessarin Valley — no difficult terrain, no getting lost, tracks left no trace); Spellcasting (1st level, 2 slots: Hunter's Mark, Fog Cloud)
- **Attack:** Longbow +6 (1d8+4 piercing, range 150/600); Shortsword +6 (1d6+4 piercing)
- **Tactical style:** Scout and ambush. Leads the approach. Opens at range. Covers exits and escape routes. Not a tank — repositions constantly. Will not waste an action on a suboptimal angle.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 34/34
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** The same as always. The chamber didn't shake her.
- **Notes:** Descended to the Pool with Zero. One of only two people who know about the lower chamber. Has said nothing about it to the rest of the party and will not unless Zero does.

## Brynn Coldmantle

**Species:** Dwarf
**Class:** Cleric (Forge Domain)
**Level:** 4
**Age:** 89 (middle-aged for a dwarf)
**Pronouns:** She/her

### Appearance
Broad and solid, with Moradin's hammer symbol worn openly at her throat and a working warhammer at her hip. Short dark hair going silver at the temples. Warm face that could also stop a door.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Steady — she is the calmest person in any given room, under any circumstances
- **Speaks like:** Plain, warm, direct. "We go in, we do the thing, we come out." Not unkind. Not complicated.
- **Moral Outlook:** Moradin's way: make things well, hold the line, don't waste what's been built. Has strong opinions about things being left in better condition than she found them.
- **Sense of Humor:** Bawdy and dry, delivered completely straight-faced.

### Backstory (Summary)
A cleric of Moradin who has spent decades in fieldwork rather than a forge-temple. Daven found her years ago on a ruin job and they've worked together intermittently since. Has seen the inside of more ancient structures than most scholars. Not surprised by much anymore.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Keep Aldric alive. Get the party back to Waterdeep intact.
- **Long-term goal:** Hasn't said. The job interested her, which is enough for now.
- **Secret (if any):** None established.

### Relationship with Zero
Straightforward goodwill. Zero is clearly capable; Brynn appreciates capability without fuss. No complications. She will take direction in the chamber from anyone who sounds like they know what they're doing.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 38
- **AC:** 18 (Chain mail + shield; Blessing of the Forge applied to armor for +1 AC, included)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Channel Divinity 1×/rest (Turn Undead; Artisan's Blessing — create a simple or martial weapon or shield overnight); Blessing of the Forge (at dawn, +1 to AC or attack/damage of one weapon); Spellcasting (WIS +5, DC 13, 1st: 4 slots, 2nd: 3 slots)
- **Spells prepared:** Cure Wounds, Healing Word, Guiding Bolt, Shield of Faith, Bless, Spiritual Weapon, Heat Metal; Domain spells always prepared: Identify, Searing Smite, Heat Metal, Magic Weapon
- **Attack:** Warhammer +5 (1d8+3 bludgeoning, versatile 1d10+3)
- **Tactical style:** Front line. Holds position between Aldric and whatever is threatening him. Uses Healing Word as a bonus action to keep others standing while staying in melee. Spiritual Weapon for sustained damage while she holds the line. Bless goes up early if there's time to prepare.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 38/38
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Easy. Ready to work.
- **Notes:** Primary assignment is Aldric. Will not leave him undefended in the chamber under any circumstances.

## Syrenne

**Species:** Human
**Class:** Bard (College of Lore)
**Level:** 5
**Age:** Mid-fifties
**Pronouns:** She/her

### Appearance
Mulhorandi woman, dark skin, close-cropped grey-white hair. Carries a thirteen-point compass rose on a cord at her neck — the same symbol as the Instrument. Moves with the deliberate economy of someone who has worked alone for a long time. Dresses for fieldwork, not presentation.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Patient — eleven years of independent research does not produce recklessness
- **Speaks like:** Precise, considered, slightly formal. Never wastes words. Occasionally uses archaic phrasing from old Mulhorandi academic tradition.
- **Moral Outlook:** The truth of the thing matters more than who controls it. Has strong opinions about what should and should not be done with power this old.
- **Sense of Humor:** Rare, quiet, usually at the expense of people who underestimate her.

### Backstory (Summary)
A Mulhorandi musicologist who spent eleven years independently tracking the Netherese resonance instrument after her former teacher, Torven Ashwick, disappeared from legitimate scholarship. Has authenticated correspondence proving Torven sought leverage over the resonance network rather than understanding. Carries primary source documentation predating any academic survey of Gharraghaur by approximately four centuries. Chose Zero specifically — the compass rose markers were placed for Zero to follow.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Return to Waterdeep with the full documentation she and Torven produced overnight. Begin the work of publication.
- **Long-term goal:** Ensure the instrument's purpose — if restoration is the right word — is not controlled by any single party, including herself.
- **Secret (if any):** The depth of her relationship with Torven before his fall from legitimate scholarship has not been fully disclosed. What exactly she witnessed, and when, remains unclear.

### Relationship with Zero
Mutual recognition of equals — possibly more. Syrenne built the trail for Zero to follow and waited. She has accepted that trust is earned on the road and is doing the work. She watches Zero's decisions carefully; so far they have held.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 30
- **AC:** 13 (Leather armor, DEX +1)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Bardic Inspiration d8 (3×/long rest); Cutting Words (reaction — when a creature makes a damage roll or succeeds on an ability check or attack roll, expend one Bardic Inspiration die and subtract the result, reducing the damage or potentially turning the success into a failure); Jack of All Trades; Font of Inspiration; strong Expertise in Arcana and History
- **Spells known:** Vicious Mockery, Minor Illusion; 1st: Dissonant Whispers, Detect Magic, Healing Word; 2nd: Suggestion, Silence; 3rd: Hypnotic Pattern, Dispel Magic
- **Spell slots:** 1st: 4, 2nd: 3, 3rd: 2
- **Attack:** Dagger +3 (1d4+1 piercing) — last resort only
- **Tactical style:** Control and information. Hypnotic Pattern to neutralize multiple threats at once. Dispel Magic prepared specifically for the resonance chamber environment. Silence to shut down casters or seal off sound in the chamber. Hangs back with Aldric when Sable and Brynn are forward. Her Cutting Words reaction is her primary contribution when not casting.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 30/30
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Quieter than before. Eleven years ended in that chamber. She is working out what comes next.
- **Notes:** Worked alongside Torven on the chamber documentation overnight. The nature of their conversation in the chamber has not been fully explored. Her assessment — "You weren't recruited for this expedition. You were convened." — has been borne out. The full shape of what she knew about the first expedition, and when she knew it, remains unexamined.

## Aldric Fenn

**Species:** Human
**Class:** Bard (pre-specialization)
**Level:** 2
**Age:** Late twenties
**Pronouns:** He/him

### Appearance
Human male, late twenties, New Olamn alumnus. Nervous energy that transforms completely when he sits down with an instrument. Carries his lute across his back and the First Key, Second Key, and Instrument in his satchel. Looks exactly like someone who should not be in a ruin.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Genuine — what he feels shows on his face; he has not mastered the gap between feeling and expression
- **Speaks like:** A little too fast when anxious; deliberate and precise when talking about music.
- **Moral Outlook:** Wants to do the right thing but is still figuring out what that means under pressure.
- **Sense of Humor:** Warm, self-deprecating.

### Backstory (Summary)
Got in over his head purchasing a mysterious antique music box at the Melvaunt estate auction. With Zero's help, discovered the box was the Netherese resonance instrument. Activated the Instrument alongside Zero — it chose its openers. Has been drawn into the expedition on the basis of that. Zero's mentorship over the past days has shifted his trajectory: he has decided to pursue the bardic track at New Olamn after this is over.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Don't die. Don't lose the First Key, Second Key, or the Instrument.
- **Long-term goal:** Return to New Olamn and pursue the bardic track in earnest. Write the piece that's been in his head since the hearthside performance.
- **Secret (if any):** None. Aldric is almost frustratingly transparent.

### Relationship with Zero
Owes Zero a significant debt across multiple counts: the retrieval job, the Velvet Alcove sanctuary, the musical mentorship, the decision about his future. Genuine affection and trust. Will do what Zero says in a dangerous situation without argument, which is both an asset and a responsibility.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 15
- **AC:** 12 (Leather armor, DEX +2)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Bardic Inspiration d6 (3×/long rest); Jack of All Trades; strong Charisma-based checks; primarily a non-combatant
- **Spells known:** Vicious Mockery, Friends; 1st: Healing Word, Charm Person
- **Spell slots:** 1st: 2
- **Attack:** Dagger +4 (1d4+2 piercing) — last resort only
- **Tactical style:** Non-combatant. Stays behind Brynn. Uses Vicious Mockery to create distance and impose disadvantage if directly threatened. Will use Healing Word on Brynn if she drops. Should never be in melee range of anything hostile — if he is, something has gone wrong.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 15/15
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Moved and purposeful. The chamber confirmed something he didn't have words for yet. He has documented everything he experienced.
- **Notes:** Carrying the First Key, Second Key, and the Instrument in his satchel. Has documented his experience of the chamber in detail. Committed to the bardic track at New Olamn. Brynn's primary protective assignment.

## Corvin Ashgate

**Species:** Tiefling
**Class:** Warlock (The Fiend)
**Level:** 3
**Age:** Approximately 20
**Pronouns:** He/him

### Appearance
Lavender skin, small swept-back horns, well-dressed without being overdressed for the road. Carries the Ashgate family calling cards. Looks younger than his responsibilities.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Earnest — he means what he says, and what he says is often more self-aware than people expect from someone his age
- **Speaks like:** Warm and direct, with occasional self-deprecating asides about his own inexperience. Not a performer. Just honest.
- **Moral Outlook:** Believes the right thing exists and can be found, and is genuinely anxious about whether he'll recognize it when it arrives.
- **Sense of Humor:** Dry, quiet, usually aimed at himself.

### Backstory (Summary)
Young tiefling heir to the Ashgate family who is financing the Gharraghaur expedition — but not merely as a patron. His grandfather, Aldric Ashgate, was a member of the first expedition to reach the chamber forty years ago; that party chose to leave the Keys and withdraw, afraid not of the song but of themselves. He left Corvin a letter sealed with the Ashgate crest and a compass rose, instructing him to make the choice as himself, not as an heir. Corvin read the letter en route to the tower and sat in the chamber his grandfather was too afraid to use and made his peace. Syrenne had records of the first expedition but had not named Aldric Ashgate as a member.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Return to Waterdeep. His grandfather's charge is discharged; the next choice belongs to him.
- **Long-term goal:** Understand what comes after the chamber — for the expedition, for his family's history, and for whatever his pact is actually connected to.
- **Secret (if any):** The precise nature of his grandfather's connection to Gharraghaur is unclear. His pact — whether inherited, chosen, or something in between — may be more entangled with the resonance network than it appears.

### Relationship with Zero
Corvin knows Zero is a changeling; Zero told him directly and trust was established on both sides. He has asked Zero to help him make the right choice at the site. That promise is live. He is the most openly vulnerable member of the party.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 22
- **AC:** 13 (Leather armor, CHA-based Defense invocation not taken; DEX +1)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Eldritch Blast +5 (1d10 force, range 120 ft.); Hellish Rebuke (reaction, 2d10 fire, DEX save DC 13); Fiendish Vigor (False Life at will); Darkvision (tiefling); Spellcasting (CHA +5, DC 13, 2 spell slots, recover on short rest)
- **Spells known:** Eldritch Blast, Minor Illusion; 1st/2nd: Armor of Agathys, Command, Hold Person
- **Attack:** Eldritch Blast +5 (1d10 force) at range; Dagger +3 (1d4+1) in desperation
- **Tactical style:** Back rank. Opens with Eldritch Blast and stays at range. Hold Person if a single target is threatening someone. Armor of Agathys if he expects to be hit. Not a frontliner — his value in the chamber is more likely to be presence and decision-making than damage output.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 22/22
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Resolved. He made the choice his grandfather couldn't. He knows it.
- **Notes:** Read his grandfather's letter en route to the tower. Spent the night in the chamber with the letter in his hands. The question of his pact's entanglement with the resonance network has not been explored.

## Mira

**Species:** Human
**Class:** Wizard (School of Divination)
**Level:** 4
**Age:** Mid-forties
**Pronouns:** She/her

### Appearance
Stout, iron-grey hair, ink-stained fingers. Carries more notebooks than weapons. Looks exactly like what she is: someone who has spent thirty years being right about things and has stopped being surprised by it.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Exacting — imprecision is not a stylistic difference, it is an error
- **Speaks like:** Clear, structured sentences. Sets working expectations up front. Will tell you exactly what she needs and exactly why.
- **Moral Outlook:** Knowledge is not neutral, but ignorance is always worse. Understanding what something is comes before deciding what to do about it.
- **Sense of Humor:** Exists, but requires patience to locate.

### Backstory (Summary)
Scholar and academic lead of the Gharraghaur expedition. Has worked with Daven before; he is one of the few field operatives she trusts to run a site correctly. Precise, exacting, not unfriendly once she's decided you're useful. Has been briefed on Torven by Daven; her reaction settled into focused professionalism rather than alarm, which may itself be telling.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Document the resonance chamber completely. Understand the Netherese resonance network from primary sources.
- **Long-term goal:** Publish findings through legitimate academic channels — and ensure no single party controls the knowledge.
- **Secret (if any):** None established. What she knows about Torven and how long she's known it has not been explored.

### Relationship with Zero
Professional respect established early and maintained. Mira set working expectations around information-sharing on the road; Zero met them. She watches Zero's handling of Torven with something that might be approval.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 24
- **AC:** 12 (DEX +2; no armor)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Portent (2 Portent dice per long rest — roll 2d20 at dawn, replace any roll made by any creature she can see with one of these results); Arcane Recovery; Spellcasting (INT +6, DC 14, 1st: 4, 2nd: 3, 3rd: 2 slots)
- **Spells prepared:** Detect Magic, Identify, Arcane Eye, See Invisibility; Shield (reaction); Scorching Ray, Fireball; Counterspell
- **Attack:** Quarterstaff +2 (1d6 bludgeoning) — she will not use this willingly
- **Tactical style:** Not a combatant by preference. In a fight, Counterspell is her most valuable action. Shield keeps her standing. Her Portent dice are best saved for the chamber itself — replacing a critical roll at a resonance site may matter far more than any combat outcome. Her primary function in Gharraghaur is investigation, translation, and analysis.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 24/24
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Absorbed. She and Torven worked the chamber walls overnight. She is still processing what they found.
- **Notes:** Daven holds Brennan's botanical specimen list; fulfilling it is Mira's responsibility (specimens to be collected on the return journey). What she and Torven discovered overnight beyond what was shared in session has not been fully explored.

## Daven

**Species:** Human
**Class:** Fighter (Battle Master)
**Level:** 5
**Age:** Late fifties
**Pronouns:** He/him

### Appearance
Lean and weathered, mostly-grey beard, economy of movement. Wears nothing he doesn't need. Has been in enough bad situations that he stopped looking surprised by them decades ago.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Reliable — not charismatic, not innovative, exactly what he says he'll be
- **Speaks like:** Terse. Subject-verb-object. Will not repeat himself. Respects the same quality in others.
- **Moral Outlook:** Do the job correctly. Protect the people who are counting on you. Everything else is philosophy.
- **Sense of Humor:** Bone-dry, deployed rarely, lands perfectly.

### Backstory (Summary)
Field lead of the expedition with personal knowledge of the Dessarin Valley and the approach to Vaester's Tower. Has visited the site before. Knew the northern blockage wasn't rockfall before anyone confirmed it. Brought in Sable and Brynn from his own network. Has kept Zero's briefing about Torven quiet and professional — told Corvin, ensured Mira was briefed, held the line on party cohesion. He has been in harder situations with worse odds.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Get everyone back to Waterdeep. The coffle threat past the Stone Bridge is still active on the return road.
- **Long-term goal:** Not disclosed. This job interests him beyond the contract, but he hasn't said why.
- **Secret (if any):** His prior visits to the Vaester's Tower area and his foreknowledge about the blockage suggest more history with this site than he has mentioned.

### Relationship with Zero
Mutual professional respect, the kind that doesn't need to be stated. Daven recognized Zero's capabilities early and has acted on that assessment consistently — taking Zero's briefing on Torven seriously, endorsing the Stone Bridge split, trusting Zero's read on Syrenne. He is probably the most reliable ally in the party in a crisis.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 48
- **AC:** 17 (Chain mail + shield)
- **Key abilities:** Action Surge (1×/rest); Second Wind (1×/rest, 1d10+5 HP); Extra Attack; Battle Master Maneuvers (4 superiority dice, d8): Riposte, Trip Attack, Commander's Strike, Precision Attack
- **Attack:** Longsword +7 (1d8+4 slashing, versatile 1d10+4); Hand Crossbow +7 (1d6+4 piercing, range 30/120)
- **Tactical style:** Controlled aggressor. Positions to protect the back rank. Uses Trip Attack to give allies advantage. Commander's Strike to hand an attack to Sable at the right moment. Action Surge when something genuinely needs to die now. Does not panic.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 48/48
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Same as always. Ready.
- **Notes:** Holds Brennan's botanical specimen list. Knows about Torven. Has not underestimated anyone on this road and is not about to start.

## Torven Ashwick

**Species:** Human
**Class:** Wizard (Divination)
**Level:** 6
**Age:** Mid-sixties
**Pronouns:** He/him

### Appearance
Older, academic bearing, the look of a man who has spent his life in libraries and knows it. Not physically imposing. Carries more than he appears to — in knowledge, in history, in things he has not said.

### Personality
- **Defining Trait:** Compartmentalized — he can hold contradictory things at once and function perfectly well; this is both his greatest intellectual strength and the quality that made what he did possible
- **Speaks like:** Precise, scholarly, occasionally warm. The warmth is real. So is the compartmentalization.
- **Moral Outlook:** Genuinely believes understanding should precede action. Also used that belief as cover for twelve years. The distinction between the two is exactly what Zero's confrontation forced him to face.
- **Sense of Humor:** Dry and self-aware, in a way that makes it harder to distrust him than it should be.

### Backstory (Summary)
Retired scholar, partial Loross translator, expert on Netherese history. Was Syrenne's teacher before he disappeared from legitimate scholarship. Confirmed as Lira's principal via authenticated letter — his actual goal was academic primacy, not political power; he cultivated Lira for three years as a mechanism. Deliberately blocked the northern approach to Gharraghaur fourteen months ago; sent a surveillance asset after the party on departure morning that Zero did not detect. Confronted by Zero on the 19th of Kythorn; gave his full confession in the chamber on the 20th. The test came and he chose grace over leverage — working alongside Mira on the chamber documentation with his whole self for the first time in thirty years.

### Agenda
- **Short-term goal:** Support the expedition's return to Waterdeep and the retrieval of the Third Key. Contribute his knowledge and translation work without agenda.
- **Long-term goal:** Publish the findings of the Gharraghaur expedition through legitimate channels — in full collaboration with Mira and Syrenne, not in competition with them. Make good use of what he nearly destroyed.
- **Unresolved:** Lira's expectations of him on return. She was his mechanism for three years; she will expect results or an accounting. How he handles that is not yet determined.

### Relationship with Zero
Transformed in the chamber. Zero heard his full confession, helped him to his feet, and called him old friend. Torven received this with the expression of a man who expected judgment and received grace and doesn't know what to do with the weight of that. He is now genuinely, uncomplicatedly allied — working alongside Mira on the chamber documentation overnight with his whole self for the first time in thirty years. He has decided what kind of person he is going to be. He intends to keep the decision.

### Combat Role
- **Max HP:** 28
- **AC:** 12 (DEX +2; no armor)
- **Key abilities/spells:** Arcane Recovery; Portent (2 dice — School of Divination; replaces any creature's roll he can see); Spellcasting (INT +7, DC 15, 1st: 4, 2nd: 3, 3rd: 3 slots)
- **Spells prepared:** Identify, Detect Magic, Comprehend Languages, Legend Lore; Counterspell, Dispel Magic; Suggestion, Modify Memory; Fireball
- **Attack:** Dagger +5 (1d4+2) — will not initiate melee
- **Tactical style:** He is not going to fight the party, and if things become violent in the chamber it will be via manipulation of the resonance environment rather than direct combat. His Counterspell and Dispel Magic are the most dangerous tools he carries — particularly if he uses them to disrupt the party's actions at the Key rather than support them. His Portent dice, like Mira's, may matter more in the chamber than in any fight.

### Current Status
- **HP:** 28/28
- **Conditions:** None
- **Mood/Disposition:** Settled. Something he was carrying for a long time has been set down. He is doing the work.
- **Notes:** Full confession given 20th of Kythorn in the chamber. Lira's expectations on return are unresolved. Does not yet know about the pool or its contents. Working with Mira on chamber documentation — producing the most honest scholarship of his career.
